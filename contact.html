About The Creators

Isabel, Emily, Feifei, & Esfa

We are four high school coders, driven by a shared passion for technology and innovation. We came together to make an informational website specifically engineered for young voters. Only the Truth was created to educate the public, specifically Gen Z, about the current election. We hope that our website will help voters with indecision by providing them with facts to help them decide on a candidate. Our journey has been filled with challenges and learning experiences, but the thrill of creating something impactful has kept us motivated.
